About Us

Florée Studio is a London-based flower studio inspired by natural beauty, craftsmanship, and timeless elegance. Since opening in 2025, we’ve been creating floral designs that feel personal, thoughtful, and effortlessly refined.

We work with seasonal blooms and trusted local growers to craft arrangements that celebrate life’s moments — from everyday gestures to once-in-a-lifetime occasions. Every bouquet is made with care, attention to detail, and a genuine love for flowers.
