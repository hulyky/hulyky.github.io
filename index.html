About Us

Welcome to Florée Studio, a flower boutique based in the heart of London. We believe that flowers are more than decoration — they are a way to express emotions, celebrate moments, and bring beauty into everyday life.

Every bouquet is handcrafted using fresh, seasonal blooms sourced from trusted local growers. Whether for a special occasion or simply to brighten your space, we’re here to help you share joy and creativity through flowers.
